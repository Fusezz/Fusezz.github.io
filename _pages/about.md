Hello! I’m Luke, and I want to be at the forefront of technology, pushing humanity forward by exploring new frontiers and building tools that take us further, whether that’s reaching space, advancing communications, or designing the next generation of innovative systems.

I design and build hands-on projects in RF, electronics, and embedded systems. From custom voice assistants to portable antennas, these experiments and creations fuel my passion for innovation and my desire to turn ideas into reality. I also have a deep love for math, science, and physics, which help to guide the way I approach problem-solving and engineering challenges. This page is where I track and showcase all of my projects, and even the occasional disaster, as a living record of my journey in engineering and technology.

My interest in engineering started when I was 13, thanks to my dad, an electrical engineer. He brought me behind the scenes at the Sun ‘n Fun airshow while working ground operations, where I got to see the full Instrument Landing Systems and the communication equipment used by air traffic controllers. That experience ignited my fascination with technology and systems that make complex operations possible.

Since then, I’ve been deeply involved in Ham Radio (my callsign is K4RIN say hi if you hear me on the air!) and have continued to grow my skills through my internship at Phoenix Navtech. My goal is to one day work with a space company or a tech company producing humanity’s cutting-edge technology, projects that truly help us evolve and explore the universe.
